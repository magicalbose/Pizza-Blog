Famous Chef Franco Pepe:



Franco Pepe is widely regarded as one of the world's greatest pizza chefs. Based in Caiazzo, a small town near Naples, Italy, he is the mastermind behind Pepe in Grani, a pizzeria that has achieved global recognition for its innovative approach to traditional Neapolitan pizza.

Pepe in Grani:

Opened in 2012, Pepe in Grani is more than just a pizzeria; it's a culinary destination. Located in a 17th century building, the restaurant offers not only incredible pizzas but also a unique dining experience. It has won numerous awards, including being named the best pizzeria in the world multiple times.
